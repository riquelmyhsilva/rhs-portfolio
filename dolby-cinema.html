biography

Born in 2006, I had my first contact with technology when I was 5 years old. I started to learn how to use the computer to access the internet and play games, since then I never stopped being interested in computers. At the age of 11, I discovered the world of programming and became interested in game development, starting my studies with the Java language, programming some simple games. At the age of 16 I started the computer technician course at Senac Education Agent, obtaining general technical knowledge of the computing world, such as hardware, networks, and programming itself. From there my programming knowledge began to evolve exponentially.

I learned how to develop websites, software and manage databases. Today I'm looking to enter the job market, and I feel very honored to develop an application to add value to your life! Let's work together?
